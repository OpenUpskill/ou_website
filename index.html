OpenUpskill

Empowering students through Open Source and Product Excellence, budding entrepreneurship

Overview

OpenUpskill is a next-generation learning and innovation community dedicated to cultivating an Indian talent pool of skilled developers, engineers, and technologists, mentored globally. Our mission is to empower students by encouraging their active contributions to the open-source ecosystem and real-world product development. Through this approach, we aim to create meaningful impact by enhancing employability and driving innovation at scale.

We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application by nurturing a student community of open-source contributors, product thinkers, and problem solvers ready to lead in the digital economy.

Vision

To shape the future of employment by empowering the Indian student community to engage in product development, foster innovation, and cultivate practical skills through active participation in open-source initiatives.

Mission

  • Empowering engineering students across all disciplines by engaging them in open-source contributions and real-world project experience
  • Promote community-driven skill development to create a collaborative learning environment globally.
  • Bridge the innovation gap by delivering customer-focused products, solutions, software, hardware etc.
  • Incubate startups with college students and enabling students to become founders and contribute to reducing unemployment in India.
