OpenUpskill

Empowering Students Through Open Source, Product Excellence & Entrepreneurship

Who We Are

OpenUpskill is a next-generation learning and innovation platform building skilled Indian developers and technologists mentored by global experts. We enable students to contribute to open-source and build real-world products—bridging the gap between theory and practice.

Vision

To shape the future of employment by empowering Indian students to build products, foster innovation, and gain practical skills through open-source engagement.

Mission

  • Empower engineering students through real-world projects and open-source work
  • Foster a global community-driven learning environment
  • Deliver impactful software, hardware, and digital solutions
  • Incubate student-led startups to reduce unemployment

Core Focus Areas

🔧 Open-Source Contribution

  • GitHub-based contributions, PR training, triaging issues
  • Structured mentorship and gamified progress tracking

🧠 Product Development & Innovation

  • Startup-style product-building simulations
  • MVP design, Agile methods, real feedback loops

📚 Upskilling Programs

  • Hands-on projects in software & hardware
  • Masterclasses: Linux, Kubernetes, CI/CD, Git & more

Our Initiatives

  • Product Labs: Sandbox for rapid prototyping
  • OSS Evangelism: Events & workshops to spread awareness
  • Talent Network: Connect top contributors with hiring startups
  • Mentor Guidance: Global experts guiding student innovators

We Work With

  • 🎓 Students & Fresh Graduates
  • 🏫 Colleges & Universities
  • 🧑‍🏫 Mentors & Experts
  • 🤝 NGOs, Hiring Managers, and OSS Communities
